Center for International Emergency Medical Services

The Center for International Emergency Medical Services (CIEMS) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in Colorado, Boston, and New York that was formed in 2006 for the purpose of improving emergency services systems worldwide. The Center is currently developing partnerships with organizations wishing to support its goals, and seeking individuals who have an interest in International Emergency Services Development.
